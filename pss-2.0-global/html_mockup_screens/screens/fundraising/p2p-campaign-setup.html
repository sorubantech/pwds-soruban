Join us for Run for Education 2026! Whether you run, walk, or cheer from the sidelines, you can help send children to school. Create your fundraising page, share with friends, and watch the impact grow.

Run for Education 2026 is our flagship peer-to-peer fundraising campaign. Last year, 150 fundraisers helped raise over $120,000 to support children's education across 12 countries.

This year, we're aiming even higher. Every dollar raised goes directly to providing school supplies, uniforms, and tuition for children who need it most.
