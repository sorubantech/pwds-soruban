Help Us Build a School for 500 Children in Rural Kenya
Hope Foundation Education

In the heart of Makueni County, Kenya, over 500 children walk more than 5 kilometers daily to attend classes in an overcrowded, crumbling structure. The building has no proper roof, no desks, and barely any supplies.

Our mission is to build a modern, 8-classroom primary school that will serve this community for generations to come. The new school will include proper classrooms, a library, clean water access, and solar-powered lighting.

Every dollar you donate brings us one step closer to giving these children the education they deserve. Join us in making this dream a reality.

Frequently Asked Questions
Where exactly will the school be built?
In Makueni County, approximately 200km southeast of Nairobi. The site was donated by the local village chief and is centrally located to serve children from 5 surrounding villages.
How will the funds be used?
85% of funds go directly to construction materials and labor, 10% to school supplies and furniture, and 5% to administrative and logistics costs.
When will the school be completed?
We aim to complete construction by December 2026, with the grand opening ceremony planned for January 2027 to coincide with the new school year.
