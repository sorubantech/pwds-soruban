Conversion Strategies

Tips & best practices
  • 5+ shifts rule: Volunteers who attend 5+ shifts are 3x more likely to donate. Prioritize engagement for newer volunteers.
  • Personal ask: Personal ask by direct manager has the highest conversion rate (33%). Equip managers with talking points.
  • Thank-you emails: Include a soft donation CTA in volunteer thank-you emails. This yields 8% click-through.
  • Donor events: Invite volunteers to donor appreciation events for crossover exposure. 22% of attendees convert within 30 days.
